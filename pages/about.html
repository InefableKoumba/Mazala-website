About Us

Born from the collaboration between two Analyst-Programmers, Mazala is a software engineering firm created in 2020 by James Adonis DJIMBY and KOUMBA Surprise Ineffable, and has been present on the new technologies market since then.

Mazala is based in UI & UX Design, Web Development, Mobile App Development, Robotics, Artificial Intelligence.

We also support Start-Ups and Companies in their computerization projects for their information system, as well as in their innovative ideas for a better experience of digitizing their needs and tasks. We set ourselves the goal of computerizing and digitizing all manual data processing systems for companies as well as the various needs or ideas of individuals that we will have the privilege of managing in order to guarantee a better experience of the digital age as well as simplicity, and reliability in the processing of data and the performance of various tasks.

We are diligent, passionate, and determined to provide you with quality work on time. We remain available to meet you in order to start this great adventure together.
